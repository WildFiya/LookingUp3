Looking Up is here to provide practical ways to infuse your life with positivity on a daily basis. When life gets hard or just extremely busy, losing our center and sometimes our cool is a slippery slope. Or maybe, times have gotten so hard it feels like you can't see the light and all the self-help options are overwhleming. Looking Up is here to offer friendly suggestions that anyone can do to shift the energy and recreate avenues of happiness and fulfillment.

Just remember ... when you get that haunting feeling that sits in the pit of your stomach ... and frustration creeps up ... and that unworthy and in-lack auto response kicks in ... remember that you don't have to feel that way.

Change Your Mind. Change Your Life.

The mind is a very powerful muscle and like our arms and legs, the mind can be trained to serve us in the ways we need. The power of thought is under estimated but extremely resourceful in most any situation. Thinking positively can be a challenge when the dust is flying but recalling simple techniques can get you through the storm.
  • Watch Your Mouth ::
    Common phrases like "I'm broke." or "I can't." reinforce a negative and incapable mindset.

  • What Did You Expect ::
    Do you expect the best? Or do you always think the worst is going to happen? Check your expectations for yourself and your life.

  • Thoughts Become Things ::
    "Thoughts become things" is a famous line from Mike Dooley and oh so true! Positive affirmations are a great way to create new thought habits. Repetition is key and these can go anywhere with you.

Love Your Body

How you treat and carry your body is very important. Do people with their arms crossed appear to be the most helpful? No probably not. Their body language gives off closed energy and so you likely seek the smiling woman on the platform to ask directions. Take note of how you treat your body throughout the day. All habits can be changed once we acknowledge them.
  • Quench Your Life ::
    The human body is made of about 60% of water and thus vital to our bodies' healthy operation. Drinking plenty of water keeps your body hydrated and your mind fresh.

  • Breathe Through It ::
    At any moment you feel yourself losing the ground below you or feel like you can't sort your thoughts .. take a breath. Actually take five breaths in a five second inhale and five second exhale pattern.

  • Work it Out ::
    The best way to exert energy, clear the head and show self-love is by exercising. The activity of your choice will get the heart going and can provide more clarity than you might expect.

  • LOOK UP ::
    Where are your eyes when you walk? When your energy is low are your eyes also hanging low? LOOK UP! It's a simple way to tell your mind and heart that you are open to better.


Spark Your Soul

We exist as the combination of physical body and internal space which many call our soul. How is your soul? Do you feed your soul? Do you make the best of every day or does time pass you quickly? What makes your soul feel good? What makes your soul feel bad? Knowking what serves your growth
and what hinders your growth are key to soul strengthening.
  • Practice Makes Happy ::
    Meditation and yoga are two practices that can be explored daily.
    The more you practice .. the happier your soul.

  • Say Thank You ::
    Our mothers taught us manners, hopefully, but did they teach you that gratefulness brings more of what you want? Even when it feels like you want the world recognizing something as simple as the breeze will remind you that the world is full of pleasure and your dreams are already yours.

  • Relieve the Pressure ::
    Laugh at yourself. Laugh at life. Laugh at the tough times. Remember that nothing is usually as bad as it seems. And that in your life ... you create your destiny.

  • Mention Intentions ::
    Set intentions .. INTENTIONALLY.
    Create a short list (1-3) of intentions to give your attention to in one week. And be sure to INTENTIONALLY think of them throughout your day. Soon, these thoughts will be on auto-pilot and you can take note of your shift. And these thoughts can reveal your next steps.
